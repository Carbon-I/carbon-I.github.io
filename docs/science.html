High-resolution Mapping of Obscured Greenhouse Gases

Carbon-I will quantify greenhouse gas (GHG) fluxes from point to global scales and deliver actionable data on emission sources.

Carbon-I fills the data hole in the humid tropics The humid tropics are hardly observed by current satellites CH4 emissions are heterogenous across the globe, highest in the tropics. Carbon-I will investigate hotspots and flux heterogeneity globally.
