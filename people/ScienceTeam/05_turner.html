Alex Turner

Atmospheric Chemistry & Carbon Cycle feedbacks

University of Washington
Github Google Scholar OrcidID Website

Alex Turner is an Assistant Professor of Atmospheric Sciences at the University of Washington where he works on atmospheric chemistry and the carbon cycle.

Education

  • PhD in Atmospheric Chemistry | Harvard University | 2012 - 2017
  • BS in Mechanical Engineering | University of Colorado at Boulder | 2008 - 2012

Professional Experience

  • 2021 - present: Assistant Professor, University of Washington
  • 2017 - 2020: Miller Postdoctoral Fellow, University of California at Berkeley
